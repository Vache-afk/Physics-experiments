Throwing Ball from Cliff Experiment

Path Equations:

\( x(t) = v_0 \cos(\theta) t \)

\( y(t) = h_0 + v_0 \sin(\theta) t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2 \)

Equations for Velocities:

\( v_x = v_0 \cos(\theta) \)

\( v_y = v_0 \sin(\theta) - g t \)

Where:

  • \( x(t) \): horizontal position (m)
  • \( y(t) \): vertical position (m)
  • \( v_x \): horizontal velocity (m/s)
  • \( v_y \): vertical velocity (m/s)
  • \( v_0 \): initial speed (m/s)
  • \( \theta \): launch angle (degrees)
  • \( h_0 \): cliff height (m)
  • \( g \): gravity (\( 9.81 \, \text{m/s}^2 \))
  • \( t \): time (s)
