Throwing Ball from Cliff Experiment

Position Equations (Projectile Motion):

\[ \text{Horizontal position: } x(t) = v_0 \cosθ \cdot t \]

\[ \text{Vertical position: } y(t) = h_0 + v_0 \sinθ \cdot t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2 \]

Velocity Equations:

\[ \text{Horizontal velocity (constant): } v_x = v_0 \cosθ \]

\[ \text{Vertical velocity (changes with time): } v_y(t) = v_0 \sinθ - g t \]

Key Results:

\[ \text{Time of flight: } t_{\text{flight}} = \frac{v_0 \sinθ + \sqrt{(v_0 \sinθ)^2 + 2 g h_0}}{g} \]

\[ \text{Maximum height: } y_{\text{max}} = h_0 + \frac{(v_0 \sinθ)^2}{2g} \]

\[ \text{Horizontal range: } R = v_0 \cosθ \cdot t_{\text{flight}} \]

Variables:
  • \( x(t) \): Horizontal position (meters)
  • \( y(t) \): Vertical position (meters)
  • \( v_x \): Horizontal velocity (m/s) - constant
  • \( v_y(t) \): Vertical velocity (m/s) - changes with time
  • \( v_0 \): Initial speed (m/s)
  • \( θ \): Launch angle (degrees from horizontal)
  • \( h_0 \): Initial cliff height (meters)
  • \( g \): Acceleration due to gravity (\( 9.81 \, \text{m/s}^2 \))
  • \( t \): Time (seconds)
